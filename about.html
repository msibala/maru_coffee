ABOUT



Born from a love of process and everyday qualtiy, we are inspired by all things analog and the simplicity of good coffee. We believe that quality does not have to be austere and that tradition can be respected as well as re-imagined. Maru is made with intention, balance and a love for the ritual of a well-made coffee.
